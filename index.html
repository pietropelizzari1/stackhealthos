The STACK Framework

Five domains. One protocol.

Every protocol is structured across five evidence-based domains that compound when addressed together. This is not a wellness checklist — it is a clinical framework.

S
Sleep Architecture

Optimizing sleep onset, duration, and quality for hormonal and cognitive recovery.

T
Training Triggers

Matching training stimulus to recovery capacity and performance goals.

A
Attention Design

Cognitive enhancement protocols for focus, deep work, and mental clarity.

C
Chemistry Optimization

Evidence-based supplementation with exact doses, timing, and interaction data.

K
Kinetic Recovery

Active and passive recovery protocols to sustain output without burnout.
